Scienceroot

The future of science is here.

We are the first blockchain-based scientific
collaboration platform and journal.

How it works?

Science Token

We are combining everything a scientist needs into one universal platform on the ethereum blockchain to create a more efficient, intuitive and transparent scientific platform.

Etherum + Science

Our scientific ecosystem will be powered by its own unique currency Science Token (ST).

Everything covered

Scienceroot will cover the main needs of every scientist.

Collaboration

Ask questions, exchange ideas, find peers, discuss research, transfer funds.

Funding

Find partners, secure funding, help others.

Journal

Publish your work and be rewarded for your contribution to the scientific community.

Everything will be accessible through one account to which only you will have the key.
